About

Startup to scale-up to exit

I've lived the full company lifecycle. Early in my career, I joined a telecoms startup as one of the first technical hires and helped build it through rapid growth to eventual acquisition — giving me first-hand experience of sell-side preparation and what acquirers look for.

At Nextgen, I joined as employee #2 and helped scale it from 3 to 200+ staff across multiple countries. Built the Mobile Roaming Financial Clearing and IOT Discount Management platforms from a blank sheet. Central to securing Tier-1 MNO clients globally.

Most recently as Managing Director of Nextgen SIMplify, I had full P&L ownership, defined the 3-year strategy, and integrated AI-driven analytics with eSIM/5G-SA support. I present quarterly to boards and work fluently with investor-style forecasting and governance.

I also spent 4 years on MoD contracts (SC Cleared), building Oracle APEX systems for defence — giving me deep understanding of government hosting, engineering standards, and orchestration requirements.

I'm equally comfortable as a hands-on technology leader in a permanent role or providing strategic support through advisory and interim engagements.
